Homm Flower - Level 10

RARE-Alchemical-Consumable-Drug-Inhaled-Poison

Source: Homebrew

Price 300 gp

Bulk L

-----

A rare flower from off continent. Few know where it's being grown, nor under what special conditions are required to make it flourish. The flower is typically steamed with incences to create a pleasant aroma.


Saving Throw DC 12 Fortitude; Onset 10 minutes; Maximum Duration 4 hours; Stage 1 +1 item bonus to saving throws against mental effects (1 hour); Stage 2 fatigued and +1 item bonus to saving throws against mental effects (1 hour); Stage 3 fatigued and stupefied 1 (1 hour); Stage 4 fatigued, frightened 2, stupefied 2 (1 hour)

Saving Throw DC 24 Fortitude; Onset 1 round; Maximum Duration 8 hours; Stage 1 +1 item bonus to saving throws against mental effects (1 hour); Stage 2 fatigued and +1 item bonus to saving throws against mental effects (1 hour); Stage 3 fatigued and stupefied 1 (1 hour); Stage 4 fatigued, frightened 2, stupefied 2 (1 hour)
